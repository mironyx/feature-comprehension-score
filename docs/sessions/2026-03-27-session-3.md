## Decisions made

- **`defaultModel` config field (not `model`):** `OpenRouterClientConfig` originally named the field `model`, but this conflicted semantically with `GenerateStructuredRequest.model` (the per-call override). Renamed to `defaultModel` to make the precedence hierarchy explicit: `request.model` (per-call) > `OPENROUTER_MODEL` env var > `DEFAULT_MODEL` constant.
- **`modelOverride` in `generateStructured`:** Destructured as `model: modelOverride` to make `modelOverride ?? this.defaultModel` self-documenting at the call site.
- **`buildLlmClient` extracted to `src/lib/api/llm.ts`:** Both service files had identical factory functions. A single shared factory is the right abstraction here; it also ensures `OPENROUTER_MODEL` is consistently applied across all callers.
- **No LLD:** Issue #112 was an adapter swap with ADR-0015 as the design reference. No LLD was written; LLD sync skipped.
